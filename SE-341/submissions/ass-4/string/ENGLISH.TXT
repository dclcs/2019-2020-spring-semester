Press <A> and <D> to move horizontally.

[inst2]
Press <W> to decelerate the falling speed.

[inst3]
Press <S> to accelerate the falling speed.

[inst4]
Approaching the heat source will gradually melt you.

[inst5]
Approaching the cold source will slow you down.

[inst6]
Crashing into stones will smash you into pieces.

[inst7]
